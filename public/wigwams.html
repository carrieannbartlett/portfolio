Children's Sleepover Parties

Our wigwam parties are so much fun, your little ones and their friends will be talking about it for months to come.

Bell Tent Hire

We will come to your home on the day of your party to set up our bell tent with your chosen interior design. We'll then return the following day to take it all away, leaving you to enjoy a stress free time!
